Experimental Rationale

This dashboard integrates a multi-modal interrogation of the Anterior Olfactory Nucleus (AON). By combining Dual-Viral Mapping (Anatomy), Monosynaptic Rabies Tracing (Connectivity), and snRNA-sequencing (Identity), we characterize the circuit's structural organization and molecular profile.

Key Findings

  • Anatomy: Interhemispheric axons originate primarily from the Dorsolateral AON and are exclusively VGLUT1+ (excitatory).
  • Connectivity: Long-range inputs synapse onto both Excitatory and Inhibitory populations.
  • Identity: Transcriptomic profiling identifies 27 distinct clusters.
